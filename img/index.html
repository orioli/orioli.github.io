Jose Berengueres, PhD

Jose Berengueres (aka /jose a-w-e-so-m-e/) received a Masters in Electrical Engineering in Barcelona and a PhD in bio-inspired robots from Tokyo Institute of Technology. He has taught Design Thinking and Business Models Innovation in California, Germany, Mexico and Dubai. He also consults on bio-inspired manufacturing. In 2014 he was an invited mentor at StartupWeekend Dubai. He currently is assistant professor at the robotics lab at UAE University where he developed the Brown Book of Design Thinking and the Art of Creativity.
